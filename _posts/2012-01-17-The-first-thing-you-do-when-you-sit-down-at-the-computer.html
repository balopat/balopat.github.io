Let me guess: check the incoming. Check email or traffic stats or messages from your boss. Check the tweets you follow or the FB status of friends.

via sethgodin.typepad.com

Another very important thought from Seth Godin. It made me think, which people does this apply to in the software industry?

On the long term this attitude leads to a state of mind where you are used to be driven by your own decisions and not (directly) driven by the incoming "pokings" of your environment.

Who can benefit from such state of mind? 

I think everyone.

Us, coders. As the code we are developing is the design itself we are designers who have to have independent, creative thinking. The software industry cries for thinking people! 

The managers. I think it is obvious - if you want to lead, be prepared in thoughts.
